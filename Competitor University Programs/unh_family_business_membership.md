# University of New Hampshire — CEO & Family Enterprise Center
**Source:** https://paulcollege.unh.edu/ceo-family-enterprise-center/membership/family-business-membership
**University:** University of New Hampshire, Peter T. Paul College of Business and Economics

---

## Overview

Program open to family-owned and operated businesses in New Hampshire, Maine, and Northern Massachusetts.

---

## Services Offered

- 7+ learning and engagement programs per year with business experts
- Program topics tailored to family business dynamics
- 8:30 AM – 1 PM program schedule (includes breakfast and lunch)
- Unlimited program attendance among all family members
- Networking and introduction opportunities
- Access to family business experts, consultants and resources
- Growing CEO Peer Groups network
- Bi-weekly family business newsletter
- Free business advising and coaching (SBDC professional advisors)
- Priority access to business engagement events
- Priority access to UNH students for employment/internships
- Family business program development input
- 10% discount on Executive Development Programs
- 10% discount on UNH Manchester MBA Program
- Discount on Family Business Magazine
- Open invitation for on-site visits and company tours

---

## Membership

- **Annual Fee:** $1,750/year per company (covers all family members and leadership)
- **Introductory Option:** $99 program fee to attend one program per calendar year
