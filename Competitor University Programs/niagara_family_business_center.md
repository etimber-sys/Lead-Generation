# Niagara University — Family Business Center
**Source:** https://www.niagara.edu/colleges/holzschuh-college-of-business-administration/business-centers-of-excellence/family-business-center/membership-benefits/
**University:** Niagara University, Holzschuh College of Business Administration

---

## Overview

Dedicated to serving the specific needs of family businesses in northwest Ohio and southeast Michigan. Focused on forums, peer interaction, and community connections.

---

## Services Offered

- Free attendance at in-person forums and virtual events
- Interaction with family and privately owned business peers
- Access to partner resources

---

## Membership Options

| Tier | Cost | Benefits |
|---|---|---|
| Company Membership | $350 | Free attendance + up to 2 additional guests at in-person forums; unlimited guests at virtual events |
| Sole Proprietorship | $175 | Free attendance for one person at in-person and virtual forums |
| In-Person Breakfast Sponsor + Membership | $1,000 | Company logo/link on website, company brochure at place settings |
| Virtual Breakfast Sponsor + Membership | $500 | Company logo/link on website |
| Guest | $50 | Individual guest attendance at in-person forums |
