# University of Denver — Bailey Program for Family Enterprise
**Source:** https://daniels.du.edu/family-enterprise/membership/
**University:** University of Denver, Daniels College of Business

---

## Overview

Join a thriving local family enterprise community to build transformational skills and connections. No prior University of Denver affiliation necessary.

---

## Services Offered

- Quarterly Roundtable events with family business leaders and experts
- Multi-day workshops
- Peer forums
- Annual Dinner Celebration
- Family business site tours
- eNewsletter (monthly)
- Feature in digital membership directory
- Educational webinars
- LinkedIn announcements
- Peer Forums with professional coaches (additional cost)

### Programming Topics
- Family harmony
- Governance
- Leadership and continuity planning
- Investment management
- Estate planning
- Social impact

---

## Membership Types

| Tier | Cost | Benefits |
|---|---|---|
| Family Member | $750/year | Six Roundtable events (4 attendees per event), discounted annual dinner, exclusive site tours, discounted workshops, monthly newsletter, directory feature, webinars, peer forums, LinkedIn recognition |
| Advisor Member | $1,500/year | Six Roundtable events (2 attendees per event), discounted annual dinner, discounted workshops, newsletter, directory feature, webinars, peer forums, LinkedIn recognition |

**Note:** Membership maintained at a 70/30 ratio of family members to advisors.
