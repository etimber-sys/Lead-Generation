# University of Louisville — Family Business Center
**Source:** https://centers.louisville.edu/family-business-center/membership
**University:** University of Louisville, College of Business

---

## Overview

Annual membership program tailored to family-owned businesses and professional service providers.

---

## Services Offered

- Peer group roundtables (CEO, next generation leaders, human resources, family leadership, non-family executives)
- Connection with family business owner community
- Peer-to-peer learning opportunities
- Exclusive networking opportunities
- Access to trusted family business advisers
- Discounts on training and resources

---

## Membership Types

- **Family Business Membership** — for family-owned businesses
- **Professional Adviser Membership** — 70/30 split of family businesses to advisers
- **Community Partners** — associations, chambers, economic development organizations

### Dues (based on annual sales)
| Annual Sales | Annual Dues |
|---|---|
| Under $1M | $1,000/yr |
| $1M – $25M | $2,500/yr |
| $25M – $100M | $3,500/yr |
| Over $100M | $5,000/yr |
