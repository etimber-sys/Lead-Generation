# University of Nevada, Reno — Family Business Program
**Source:** https://www.unr.edu/nevada-today/news/2025/family-business-program
**University:** University of Nevada, Reno — Ozmen Center for Entrepreneurship, College of Business

---

## Overview

Newly launched program (2025) addressing challenges unique to family-owned businesses, including leadership succession, multi-generational dynamics, and AI/technology adaptation.

---

## Services Offered

- Regular networking events
- Symposiums and educational workshops
- Webinars (e.g., AI and advanced technologies for family businesses)
- In-person symposiums
- Student club and internship opportunities
- Support and resources for family-owned businesses
