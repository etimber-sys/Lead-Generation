# Seattle University — Family Business Exchange (FBX)
**Source:** https://www.seattleu.edu/business/student-life/iec/fbx/join/
**University:** Seattle University, Albers School of Business & Economics

---

## Overview

The Family Business Exchange (FBX) connects family business owners with experts, peers, and university resources through events, workshops, and exclusive member benefits.

---

## Services Offered

- **Evening Seminars** — Prominent family business owners and leading experts on succession planning, leadership development, governance; includes social happy hour and food
- **Roundtable Lunches** — Interactive workshops
- **Webinars Library** — Live and recorded content on wealth management, accounting, family governance
- **Seattle University President's Club Membership** — Exclusive university events and receptions
- **SU Lemieux Library** borrowing privileges
- **Complimentary campus parking pass**
- **MBA Project Consulting** — No-cost MBA student team consulting on business projects
- **Law School Clinics and Resources** — Free Seattle University School of Law programs: employment law, mediation, dispute resolution
- **Members-only LinkedIn Group**
