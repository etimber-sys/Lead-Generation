# University of St. Thomas — Family Business Center
**Source:** https://business.stthomas.edu/centers-institutes/family-business-center/
**University:** University of St. Thomas, OPUS College of Business

---

## Overview

Leading resource for family business education, connection, and support. Community has grown 50% in 3 years with 100+ member families. Serves Minnesota and the Upper Midwest.

---

## Services Offered

### Family Business Breakfast Series (Flagship Program)
- ~190 attendees per session
- Meets Thursdays, 7:30–9:30 a.m. on St. Paul campus
- Topics: Leadership Communication, Preparing Future Owners, Live Case Studies, Role Strengthening, Integrating Outsiders, Succession Lessons, Family Disagreement Resolution

### Peer Groups
- 6 groups: one for executives, two for women, three for next generation
- Meet 7–10 times/year, up to 3 hours each
- Cost: $950/year for members, $1,250 for non-members

### Family Business Courses
- **Generational Continuity & Family Alignment** — 3 days over 4-month period
- Teaches: effective family meetings, unified decisions, ownership oversight strategies

### Advisor Engagement Luncheons
- Topics: Voices Across Generations, Governance with Purpose, Sale of Business
- Individual attendance: $60 each
- Table host: $4,000/year

### Additional
- Members-only events beyond the Breakfast Series
- Advisor Directory ("Find an Advisor")
- Three-year pilot program for East Side St. Paul families (funded by McNeely Foundation)
