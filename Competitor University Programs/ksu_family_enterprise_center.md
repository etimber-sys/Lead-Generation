# Kennesaw State University — Family Enterprise Center
**Source:** https://campus.kennesaw.edu/colleges-departments/coles/centers/family-enterprise-center/membership/index.php
**University:** Kennesaw State University, Coles College of Business

---

## Overview

"Where family businesses belong" — serving family business leaders, multigenerational enterprises, and professionals serving family enterprises.

---

## Services Offered

- Access to all educational programs at no additional cost
- Monthly workshops and webinars
- Quarterly mini classes
- Semiannual panel discussions
- Closed peer groups for next-gen and emerging leaders
- Open roundtables on business growth, legacy, family dynamics
- Invitations to in-person and virtual networking events
- Member-only pricing on special events
- 10% discount on Kennesaw State University Continuing Education programs
- Recognition in monthly events email
- Affiliate Directory listing (for affiliate members)
- Opportunity to lead webinars or write articles (for affiliate members)
- Co-branded white paper publishing opportunities
- Premium opportunities: lead workshops, host panels (additional fee)

---

## Membership Types

| Tier | Cost | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Community Member | $1,000/year | Full access to programs, peer engagement, networking |
| Legacy Circle Member | $5,000/year | All community benefits + funds one scholarship membership + newsletter recognition + scholarship committee eligibility + speaking priority |
| Affiliate Member | $1,000/year | For professionals: consultants, attorneys, financial planners, coaches |
