# University of Toledo — Family Business Center
**Source:** https://www.utoledo.edu/centers/family-business-center/join.html
**University:** University of Toledo

---

## Overview

Helping family businesses grow, endure, and succeed across generations through peer-learning, programming, and a trusted community.

---

## Services Offered

- Peer-learning opportunities and small group engagement
- Programs supporting family dynamics and business strategy
- University-backed education and expert-led programming
- Trusted and confidential spaces
- Three Community Events (signature gatherings)
- Monthly Info Sessions (niche, content-rich topics)
- Executive-in-Residence (EIR) Program (exclusive, hand-selected cohort, donor-funded)
- Five Roundtables: Manufacturing, Women/Wisdom/Wine, Leading Generation, Rising Generation, Human Resources
- Access to NWO M&A Club (mergers, acquisitions, growth, succession)
- Student Engagement (business, engineering, athletics)
- Network expansion and connections
- Curated resources for family and business success
- Optional: Affinity Group Program (additional fee)
- Optional: Certificate Programs (additional fee, some qualify for Ohio TechCred)

---

## Membership Options

| Tier | Cost | Benefits |
