# Lead Generation — LMU Family Business Program

Research and strategy project for building a lead generation system for the LMU Family Business Program.

## Project Structure

- **LMU Family Business Program/** — Research on LMU's existing programs, events, LinkedIn, Instagram, and newsroom presence
- **Competitor University Programs/** — Analysis of competitor family business centers (DU, KSU, Niagara, Seattle U, St. Thomas, UNH, UNR, U of L, UToledo)
- **Research/** — Strategy documents covering SEO keywords, lead magnets, multi-step forms, social proof, GA4 tracking, personas, and Clay.com capabilities
- **Project Documents/** — PRD, capstone documentation, compiled family business data (CSV), and lead gen flowchart

## Key Documents

- `Project Documents/Product Requirements Document (PRD)` — Full project requirements
- `Project Documents/Family-Businesses Compiled.csv` — Compiled lead data
- `Research/family_business_personas.md` — Target audience personas
- `Research/seo_keywords_strategy.md` — SEO strategy
- `family_business_program_analysis.html` — Program analysis report
