# Lead Generation — LMU Family Business Program

12-month growth and quality improvement strategy for the LMU Family Business Entrepreneurship Program. Goal: 8–15 net new memberships, starting from low LA market awareness with 41 confirmed leads.

## Strategic Architecture

Three parallel tracks feed into program quality improvements that drive retention and word-of-mouth.

- **Track A — Outbound:** One-time activation of 41 compiled leads (22 LMU alumni + 19 non-alumni) via persona-segmented LinkedIn + email sequences. CTA is always a free, low-barrier touchpoint — not a membership ask.
- **Track B — Referral & Partner Network:** Referral engine via current members ($150 credit), LMU CBA alumni, and professional advisors (CPAs, estate attorneys, wealth managers) who already serve family businesses.
- **Track C — Content & SEO:** Durable inbound via LA-specific SEO pages, a biweekly newsletter, and pillar content mapped to the top 10 family business pain points.

Core insight: family businesses require 3–5 touchpoints before committing. Every track feeds a trust-building nurture sequence before asking for membership.
