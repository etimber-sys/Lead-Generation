## Recommended Page Structure for SEO

1. **Location-specific pages** (not generic "About Us")
   - Include city/region name in page title
   - Mention local landmarks, neighborhoods, LA business community
   - Local customer testimonials

2. **Service/program pages** (one page per core offering)
   - Membership page
   - Events page
   - Annual conference page
   - Peer groups page

3. **Schema markup:** Business type, address, events, FAQ

4. **Technical:** Mobile-first, under 3-second load time, SSL

5. **Google Business Profile:** Optimize with photos, events, descriptions
