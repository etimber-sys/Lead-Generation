## Programs

### [Hilton Distinguished Entrepreneur Lecture Series](https://cba.lmu.edu/centers/entrepreneurship/centerprograms/hiltondistinguishedentrepreneurlectureseries/)
Brings outstanding entrepreneurs from all fields to campus to share their inspiring life and work experiences with students, alumni, faculty and staff.

### [Dinner with a Winner](https://cba.lmu.edu/centers/entrepreneurship/centerprograms/dinnerwithawinner/)
Established in 2008, Dinner with a Winner affords Diamond in the Rough awardees and a few, select, highly-motivated entrepreneurship students the opportunity to interact with successful entrepreneurs over dinner.

### [New Idea Pitch Competition](https://cba.lmu.edu/centers/entrepreneurship/centerprograms/newideapitchcompetition/)
Open to all LMU students, participants pitch their new business ideas in front of a panel of judges and a large and rowdy audience (think American Idol!) and compete for cash prizes. Sponsored by the Donald and Joyce Nores Entrepreneurial Endowment.

### [LMU Startup Weekend](https://cba.lmu.edu/centers/entrepreneurship/centerprograms/startupweekend/)
Each fall, LMU's Entrepreneurship Program hosts a Startup Weekend to provide an exciting opportunity for students to sharpen their skills in formulating and launching a business concept. The event also allows students to collaborate with professionals and experts in the business community.

### [Company Tours](https://cba.lmu.edu/centers/entrepreneurship/centerprograms/companytours/)
Entrepreneurship students get access to the most innovative and remarkable companies in the Los Angeles area. Past company visits include Google, YouTube, SpaceX, Idealab, Mad Dog Athletics and Houdini Enterprises, among others.

### [LMU Entrepreneurship Society](https://www.facebook.com/groups/236656109820325/)
The LMU Entrepreneurship Society provides students with an environment where they are able to learn and engage in the world of entrepreneurship with fellow students, the academic community and business community. Members meet on a weekly basis to hear speakers, learn from each other, share internship and job leads, and just to have fun. All majors welcome.

### [Save It Forward Initiative](https://cba.lmu.edu/centers/entrepreneurship/centerprograms/financialliteracy/)
Financial literacy initiative with a Summer 2026 Program, dedicated Leadership Team, and structured Curriculum.
