Join us for the LMU Family Business Forum: Next Generation Leadership — a candid conversation designed to bring those dynamics into the open. You'll hear directly from next-generation family business leaders navigating responsibility, identity, and leadership in real time, alongside research-informed facilitation that helps families move from tension to clarity. This forum goes beyond theory to focus on what families are actually experiencing — how emerging leaders prepare to step in, how trust is built (or tested), and how the next chapter takes shape across generations.
